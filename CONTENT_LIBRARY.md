# Team Crafties Content Library

A cozy working shelf for launch-season pattern batches, project ideas, and future printable collections.

## Content promise

Every Team Crafties content piece should feel:

- Beginner-safe: tiny steps, ordinary materials, no perfection pressure.
- Free-first: patterns, prompts, templates, and tips are given generously.
- Photo-friendly: each idea should be easy to show with a bright finished-project picture.
- Kindness-led: every page should invite encouragement before advice.
- Community-ready: include a gentle sharing prompt for future comments/uploads.
