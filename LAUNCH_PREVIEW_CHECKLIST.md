## Quick Self-Check Before Sharing

Open `index.html` in Chrome, then check:

- [ ] The Crafties homepage opens and feels welcoming.
- [ ] The purple hippo logo appears.
- [ ] The **EN / FR** language toggle works on the homepage.
- [ ] The Start Here section explains what to do next.
- [ ] The 9 featured beginner patterns are easy to find.
- [ ] Clicking a featured pattern opens its pattern page.
- [ ] Pattern pages have Materials, Steps, a kindness idea, and buttons.
- [ ] Pattern pages switch between English and French.
- [ ] The Back to Library button returns to the pattern library.
- [ ] The page still looks okay on a phone-sized window.

## Ask 2–3 Trusted Previewers

Send the preview only to people who will be kind and practical. Ask them to spend 5 minutes and answer these questions:

1. What did you think Crafties was for within the first 10 seconds?
2. Was it obvious where to start?
3. Did the English/French toggle make sense?
4. Which of the 9 beginner projects would you actually try first?
5. Did anything feel confusing, too busy, or hard to read?
6. Did it feel warm and encouraging?
7. What one small thing would make it better?

## Suggested Message to Previewers

Hi! I’m making a friendly little arts-and-crafts website called **Crafties**. It is still an early preview, but I’d love your kind first impression.

Could you take about 5 minutes and tell me:

- Is it clear what the site is about?
- Is it clear where a beginner should start?
- Does the English/French switch feel easy?
- Which beginner pattern would you try first?
- Is anything confusing or hard to read?

Thank you so much 💜

## Do Not Worry About Yet

These can wait until after the first preview:

- Translating/polishing all 91 pattern pages.
- Real user accounts or uploads.
- Comments, likes, or community moderation tools.
- Domain name and hosting decisions.
- Perfect final wording everywhere.
- Advanced pattern filters or categories.

## Next Build Priorities After Feedback

1. Fix any confusing homepage wording found by previewers.
2. Improve any pattern page that previewers found unclear.
3. Decide the simplest contact/join flow for early visitors.
4. Prepare a shareable hosted preview when Nath/Poste is ready.
5. Later: translate and polish the remaining 82 pattern pages.
