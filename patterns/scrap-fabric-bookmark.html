Materials

  • Fabric scrap
  • Needle and thread
  • Pins or clips
  • Small scissors
  • Optional sticker or label

Gentle steps

  1. Cut the fabric shape slowly and keep the edges simple.
  2. Fold or pin any edges that need to stay neat.
  3. Use small steady stitches around the shape.
  4. Add one decorative stitch, button, patch, or embroidered detail.
  5. Finish your scrap fabric bookmark with a tiny Team Crafties kindness detail or note.
