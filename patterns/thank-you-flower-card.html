Materials

  • Blank card or folded paper
  • Pencil and pen
  • Markers or watercolor
  • Scrap paper petals
  • Glue stick

Gentle steps

  1. Fold your card and lightly draw one big friendly flower.
  2. Color the center first, then add petals one by one.
  3. Glue on scrap paper petals if you want extra texture.
  4. Write one specific thank-you sentence inside.
  5. Add a tiny Team Crafties sparkle, heart, or hippo-color dot on the back.
