Crochet Plant Pot Cover

A soft, chunky crochet cover that gives a plain plant pot a cozy handmade sweater. Measure against your own pot as you go, and let the handmade charm be part of the magic.

🧶 Yarn / CrochetBeginner-friendlyBy NathFree printable pattern
💜 Tip from Nath’s craft table: keep placing the crochet base against your pot as you work. Pots vary, and handmade covers are allowed to be a little cozy and wobbly.
