Materials

  • Candle jar or practice jar
  • Paper label or tag
  • String or ribbon
  • Markers
  • Optional sticker or label

Gentle steps

  1. Choose a jar or tag and keep paper decorations away from flame areas.
  2. Measure a label or band that sits safely on the outside.
  3. Decorate with simple borders, names, flowers, or cozy words.
  4. Attach the label smoothly and tie a separate tag if wanted.
  5. Finish your tea-light paper tray with a tiny Team Crafties kindness detail or note.
